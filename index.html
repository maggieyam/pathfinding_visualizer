About

AlgoViz

AlgoViz is a tool that allows users to visualize how a computer interprets
different algorithmic paths throughout the search process.

To start a new game:

Note: Please wait until the animation has end before resetting.

  1. Select an algorithm from the dropdown menu

  2. Click to place trip_origin (start point).

    Click again to deselect.

  3. Click to place (end point).

    Click on grid to reset end point.

  4. Click to begin.

  5. Click to clear the canvas.
